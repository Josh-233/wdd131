About Nigeria

Nigeria, located in West Africa, is the most populous country on the continent. Known as the "Giant of Africa," it is rich in cultural diversity, natural resources, and vibrant traditions.

Famous Landmarks

  • Zuma Rock – the iconic natural monolith near Abuja
  • Olumo Rock – historic site in Abeokuta
  • Yankari National Park – wildlife reserve with hot springs
